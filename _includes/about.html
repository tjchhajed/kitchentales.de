I saw Shuro in the lectures at the university. I always thought of him as a handsome gentleman, too intelligent for me. My admiration for Shuro rose when I saw him playing the piano in the canteen. We exchanged short glances, with a desire to see again. We did not have to wait very long because to our surprise we met in the same German language class where our eyes could meet often because we used to sit opposite to each other. But it felt to me that Shuro never returned me smiles. He was rather ignoring me. Then I decided to unfriend him from my Facebook! But he got lucky because soon we actually ‘met’ and ‘talked’ for the first time in Chemnitz city fest with other friends. I realised that Shuro was trying hard to take his eyes off me. Well this confirmed that he felt for me but he was too proud to admit it.
